About DAPMS

DAPMS is designed as a modern departmental academic management solution to reduce manual work, improve transparency, and centralize key academic processes into one digital platform.

It supports term-based academic organization, assignment workflows, attendance reports, marks entry, result approval, and student performance tracking in a professional way.
